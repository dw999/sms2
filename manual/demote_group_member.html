3.9 Group admin - Demote group administrator
If you are group administrator or system administrator, you have right to demote group administrator to common group member. Select 'Demote Admin' on group menu (see figure 3.3.1), screen like figure 3.9.1 will be displayed. All group administrator(s) except yourself will be listed out, just select the check-box in front of the group administator(s) you want to demote, and then click the 'Demote' button. If the process successes, demoted group administrator(s) will be shown on the group member list with empty 'Role' value.

Note:
System administrator has equivalent rights of group administrator, even he/she is common member of a group. Therefore, system administrator could demote all group administrators to common members in a message group, and let it without group administrator.
