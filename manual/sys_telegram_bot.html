4.5.6 Telegram bot
Do you still remember section "2.4 Let Telegram help"? If you need notification service provided by Telegram instant messaging system, you need to apply a Telegram bot from Telegram "Bot Father". I am not intend to show you the detail steps to apply a Telegram bot, it is out of scope of this guide. If you don't know how to apply a new Telegram bot, just click the link "Bots: An Introduction for Developers" on figure 4.5.6.1 to get detail information.

I here assume you have already applied your Telegram bot successfully, so you have your bot's name, username and HTTP API token. To add Telegram bot data to SMS, select item 'System Settings' on main menu (figure 2.1.3), then click on the button 'Telegram Bot' (figure 4.5.1.1), screen like figure 4.5.6.1 will be displayed, then you can put related data of your Telegram bot into fields of figure 4.5.6.1. To save your Telegram bot data to SMS, click 'Save' button.

If you need to update your Telegram bot data, select item 'System Settings' on main menu, then click on the button 'Telegram Bot'. Your Telegram bot data will be shown on screen like figure 4.5.6.1. Just modify your data, and click 'Save' button to update your Telegram bot record on SMS.
